The Blurred Line between Work and Instance

In the history of bibliographic theory, there is some controversy about having a seperate, more abstract representation of a creative artifact beside the object itself. In FRBR and BIBFRAME, a distinction is made between the creative artifact (FRBR's Work and Expression, BIBFRAME's Creative Work) and the actual embodiment of the creative artifact (FRBR's Manifestation and Item, BIBFRAME's Instance).

In contrast, two of the most popular metadata standards, Dublin Core and Schema.org, do not differenciate between the artifact itself and the abstract representation modeled on a work.
